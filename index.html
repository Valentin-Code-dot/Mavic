What is Mavic 2 Pro?

The Mavic 2 Pro drone is an engineering marvel ideal for aerial photography. Equipped with all the best DJI technology, the drone will transform the world of aerial photography.

The Mavic 2 Pro features the all-new Hasselblad L1D-20c camera. The L1D-20c is powered by Hasselblad's unique Natural Color Solution (HNCS) 5 technology, allowing users to capture stunning aerial images with 20 megapixel resolution and stunning colors.
